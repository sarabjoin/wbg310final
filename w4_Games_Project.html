A few of my favorite games

  • Eldritch Horror
  • World of Warcraft
  • Spirit Island

Eldritch Horror

Eldritch Horror is a co-op board gamed based off of H.P.Lovecraft's works. This game features some of his scarriest monsters such as Cathulu, Yig, and Nyarlathotep. Hidden within the cards as you play are even The King in Yellow, cultists, and so much more. Players choose from a variety of characters with different abilities to team up to defeat the monsters.

World of Warcraft

Yep, that one. One of, if not THE, most well known MMORPG. In WoW you can fly around on mounts, complete achievements and quests, raid, and complete Mythic+ dungeons to improve your skills. With 100s of hours of game time, it is impossible to get bored with this game. Group up with friends and family even to create some unforgettable moments.

Spirit Island

Spirit Island is another co-op board game. Competing against other players all the time getting you down? Too many arguments arising during gameplay? Try this co-op game and team up as strong spirits who work together to take out the enemy forces invading the lands. With many characters to choose from, all with different abilities, each round of the game will continually be something new. Included are ways to increase this games difficulty as well as expansion packs to add in more content.
